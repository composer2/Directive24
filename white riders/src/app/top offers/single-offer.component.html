Customer Questions & Answers


Question: my son is 6 yrs old and about 50 lbs, will 95 cm work well or too big?

Answer: He will be fine. This is made to pretty much go straight. Hard to ride an edge on it


Question: Do the straps adjust

Answer: Yes, there is a ratcheting buckle that allows adjustment for correct fit.


Question: How do I know what size to buy?? It says 95cm, 95-cm, 120cm, 120-cm, what do the dashes (-) mean?. It will be for a 10 year old that weighs 70-75lbs.

Answer: That dash doesn't mean anything. It's either 95 or 120 cm. The 120 cm is better for your son, but frankly, my son used it once and haven't touched it since. I should have gotten him a real (used) snowboard.


Question: Is there a max weight limit?

Answer: Thank you for your inquiry. These are beginner snowboards for kids. The 120cm board should work fine for kids in the 9-12 range, and up to around 100 lbs.
